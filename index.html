About Sattvayog

Our Philosophy

Sattvayog is founded on the principle of "Sattva" - purity, harmony, and balance. We believe yoga is not just a physical practice but a holistic approach to wellness that nurtures mind, body, and spirit.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make authentic yoga accessible to everyone through personalized online classes that honor traditional practices while adapting to modern lifestyles. We aim to create a supportive community where practitioners of all levels can grow and thrive.
